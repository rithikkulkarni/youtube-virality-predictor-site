Want to understand the research behind this model?

This model is built on state-of-the-art techniques in image & natural language processing combined with a classic machine learning pipeline. By analyzing vast amounts of video metadata, it learns to predict the potential virality of new videos using only pre-publication data that have scientifically-backed influence on users' clickthrough rate.
